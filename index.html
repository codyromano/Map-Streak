Trip Ideas

Stowe, Vermont

Stowe Mountain Resort

The ski and snowboarding resort is situated near Mt. Mansfield, Vermont's tallest mountain. Its powdery trails are collectively known as "The Great 48." Nearby restaurants offer fresh pancakes with authentic Vermont maple syrup.

Hellertown, Pennsylvania

Lost River Caverns

This 250,000-year-old limestone cavern has five chambers, with crystals, that can be explored on foot. Besides stalagmite and stalactite formations, you'll see mineral growths and rock formations that seem to defy gravity.
